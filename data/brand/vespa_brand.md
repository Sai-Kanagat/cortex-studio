# Vespa — Brand Brief (demo seed)

Vespa, by Piaggio, is an icon of Italian design and urban mobility since 1946. The
Vespa Elettrica brings the classic silhouette into zero-emission electric riding.

## Positioning
- Timeless Italian design and heritage; instantly recognizable silhouette.
- Effortless, silent, zero-emission freedom through the city.
- Style and joy of the ride first, not technical specs or price.

## Audience
- Young urban professionals (25-35) in cities like Milan and Rome.
- Design-conscious commuters tired of traffic, noise, and parking.
- Sustainability-minded first-time scooter buyers.

## Brand voice
- Iconic, stylish, effortless, optimistic. A touch of la dolce vita.
- Avoid: aggressive discounting, spec-sheet language, fear-based messaging.

## Do / Don't
- DO lead with heritage, style, silence, and city freedom; make test-ride booking feel effortless.
- DON'T compete on price; DON'T bury the icon under technical jargon.

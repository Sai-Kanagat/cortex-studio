# Sai Energy System — Brand Brief (seed)

Sai Energy System is an authorized Indane (LPG) distributor operating in Coimbatore
for over 25 years. Core promise: reliable, safe cooking gas with trusted local service.

## Positioning
- Trust built over decades of local service; families know the Sai Energy name.
- Safety first: correct handling, timely inspections, genuine cylinders.
- Convenience: fast refills, doorstep delivery, and app-based booking.

## Audience
- Coimbatore households, primarily homemakers and family decision-makers.
- Small eateries and local businesses needing dependable commercial LPG.

## Brand voice
- Warm, trustworthy, local. Plain Tamil/English. Safety-conscious, never alarmist.
- Avoid: aggressive discounting language, fear-mongering, anything implying unsafe shortcuts.

## Do / Don't
- DO emphasize reliability, safety, local trust, ease of app booking.
- DON'T make price the lead message; DON'T imply competitor cylinders are dangerous.
